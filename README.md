# Yorozuya — a “I hate LinkedIn” job portal (v1.0)

Yorozuya is my personal job portal / playground project built because I *hate* LinkedIn and most job apps. Like, I hate using them, I hate them a lot.  

So I made my own thing: customizable, easy for **me** to fetch jobs and stay updated, without dealing with the usual job-app ass experience.

**Live:** https://yorozuya.dizziedbliss.dev  

> Heads-up: first load can take ~10s because it has to go to the serverless Railway thing and kick its ass to wake up from sleep. (No loading animation yet. Yeah. I know.)
> 

---

## What it does (v1.0)

- Fetches job listings from a few of my favorite companies (Discord, GitLab, Reddit, and such)
- Only shows stuff I actually care about:
    - **SRE**
    - plus a few other DevOps-ish roles
- Uses the **Greenhouse API** (hehe)

---

## Why it exists

- I wanted a job portal that matches *my* brain and lets me customize the experience.
- I’m using this app as a playground to experiment while I learn more:
    - DevOps
    - frontend
    - backend
    - basically everything

## DevOps / deployment stuff I’ve learned while building this
